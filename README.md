# AI Change Management - NIST AI RMF 1.0
### MedCore Health Systems (Fictional Organization)

An applied GRC project bringing the **NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)** to a fictional mid-size healthcare organization navigating AI adoption. It produces the governance, policy, risk, and operational artifacts a real program would need.

By [D'Arcy Bracken](https://www.linkedin.com/in/darcyvbracken). Cybersecurity and Information Assurance, B.S. in progress (WGU).

---

## Project Summary

MedCore Health Systems is adopting AI tools across clinical and administrative workflows, but without governance, unauthorized AI use is already creating HIPAA exposure. This project maps MedCore's AI risk landscape and produces the governance, policy, and operational documents needed to manage AI securely across its full lifecycle.

**Core framework:** NIST AI RMF 1.0 (GOVERN → MAP → MEASURE → MANAGE)
**Regulatory overlays:** HIPAA, HITECH
**Key risk:** Shadow AI (staff using public LLMs with PHI)
