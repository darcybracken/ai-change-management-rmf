# MC-RISK-AI-001 - AI Risk Register

**MedCore Health Systems**
*Internal Risk Document - Version 1.0*


**How to read this register**

**Likelihood:** 1 (Rare) - 5 (Almost Certain) | **Impact:** 1 (Negligible) - 5 (Catastrophic) | **Inherent Risk Score** = Likelihood x Impact (before controls) | **Residual Risk Score** = Likelihood x Impact (after controls) | Risk appetite threshold: scores above **12** require AGC escalation, scores above **16** require immediate treatment.

| **Inherent risk score**   | 9                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Existing controls**     | PatientFlow not yet deployed, red team testing (MC-RT-AI-001) required before authorization                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **Control effectiveness** | Moderate - red team testing will identify but not eliminate injection risk                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Residual likelihood**   | 2                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Residual impact**       | 3                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Residual risk score**   | 6 - Medium                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Treatment**             | Mitigate - complete prompt injection red team testing before any patient-facing LLM deployment, implement output anomaly monitoring, human escalation for any clinical routing decision                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Owner**                 | IT Security + CISO                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Next review**           | Upon red team completion                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |

### RI-008 - Staff Over-Reliance on AI Clinical Output (All Clinical AI)

| Field                     | Detail                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Risk ID**               | RI-008                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **System**                | SE-001 DiagnoAI, SA-001 Shadow AI clinical use                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| **NIST AI RMF**           | MAP 5.1 (limitations), MANAGE 4.2 (awareness)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **HIPAA**                 | 45 CFR 164.306(a)(1) - Patient safety implication                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| **Risk description**      | Clinical staff treat AI output as authoritative rather than advisory. Over time, confirmation bias reinforces AI recommendations, thereby excluding independent clinical judgment. This phenomenon is referred to as “automation bias,” the inclination to excessively trust automated systems. A model with even a 95% accuracy rate will be erroneous in one of every twenty cases, and if staff stop verifying, those instances will remain undetected. |
| **Likelihood**            | 4 - Likely (automation bias is a documented human factor in clinical AI, increases with familiarity and time pressure)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Impact**                | 3 - Moderate (patient safety, malpractice, regulatory)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Inherent risk score**   | 12 - AGC escalation threshold                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **Existing controls**     | HITL mandatory for all clinical AI, AI AUP Section 6.3 (no safety-critical decisions without clinician review)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| **Control effectiveness** | Moderate - policy exists, behavioral compliance requires training and monitoring                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **Residual likelihood**   | 2                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| **Residual impact**       | 3                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| **Residual risk score**   | 6 - Medium                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **Treatment**             | Mitigate - role-specific training on automation bias and AI limitations, CMO to reinforce in clinical communications; periodic audits of HITL compliance                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Owner**                 | CMO + HR + CISO                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| **Next review**           | Post-training (Q3 2026)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
